Mark DeMarco Timeline

  • 2012 - He released his first LP "Rock and Roll Night Club".
  • 2012 - Record label was impressed by his LP and allowed him to release his first full-length album titled "2" the same year.
  • 2014 - On April 1, 2014 he released his second full-length album "Salad Days". Both his first and second album recived "Best New Music" designation from Pitchfork.
  • 2015 - He released his third album "Another One". It was not recived as good as he's previous albums but in my humble opinion it's one of his best albums.
  • 2015 - On July 8, 2015, DeMarco released a 9-track instrumental album titled Some Other Ones and called it a "BBQ soundtrack".
  • 2017 - On May 5, 2017 DeMarco released his third studio album "This Olf Dog"
  • 2019 - On May 10, 2019 Mac released his forth album "Here Comes the Cowboy" on his own label "Mac's Record Label"
  • 2023 - On January 20, 2023 DeMarco released and instrumental album "Five Easy Hot Dogs". he fourteen-track album was recorded in different locations during a road trip from Los Angeles to New York City.
  • 2023 - On April 21, 2023 DeMarco released his first compilation album titled "One Wayne G", online only, without any prior announcement. The compilation has been noted for being unusually long, with 199 tracks and a runtime of 8:43:20
